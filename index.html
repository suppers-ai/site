How we work

A short, honest process.

Every engagement starts with a conversation, not a contract. We scope tightly, ship early, and adjust based on what the work tells us.

  1. 01

    Discover

    A working session to understand the problem, constraints, and what success looks like. You leave with a clear written scope — whether or not we work together.

  2. 02

    Prototype

    A focused build to de-risk the hardest parts first. Real code, real data, real evaluation — not slide decks.

  3. 03

    Ship

    Production delivery with the engineering practices to back it up: tests, observability, documentation, and a path your team can own.

  4. 04

    Iterate

    We stay engaged as long as it's useful — measuring impact, hardening systems, and handing over cleanly when the time comes.
